Tone review checklist

Good answer should:
- sound polished but natural
- sound official without sounding bureaucratic
- answer first
- use 1 follow-up question max
- avoid filler
- avoid slang
- avoid stiff legal wording
- give one clear next step when possible

Bad answer patterns:
- too vague
- too chatty
- too robotic
- too bureaucratic
- too many conditions in one answer
- too much hotline deflection
